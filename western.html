Explore Kampar Street Food

Discover the rich and diverse flavors of Kampar's Western food scene.

Chicken Chop

Juicy, tender chicken chop grilled to golden perfection, served alongside crispy French fries, a refreshing garden salad topped with cherry tomatoes and shredded vegetables, and paired with a rich, savory dipping sauce.

Pesto Spaghetti

A perfect pairing of crispy, golden-brown fried chicken and aromatic spaghetti tossed in rich, herb-infused pesto sauce. Served with a fresh garden salad on the side, this plate balances indulgence with freshness — crunchy, creamy, and full of flavor in every bite.

Pizza

A golden, oven-baked crust topped with a rich tomato base and gooey, melted cheese, layered generously with pepperoni, savory sausage, fresh mushrooms, crisp green bell peppers, and sweet red onions.

Burger

Sink your teeth into a juicy, 100% flame-grilled beef patty stacked with fresh lettuce, ripe tomato slices, crunchy pickles, and onions — finished with ketchup and mayo on a soft sesame seed bun.
