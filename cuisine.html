Explore Kampar Street Food

Discover the rich and diverse flavors of Kampar's Cuisine food scene.

Nasi Lemak

A fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk, typically served with sambal, fried anchovies, and a hard-boiled egg.

Roti Canai

A popular, pan-fried flatbread, typically associated with Malaysia, but with roots in India.

Char Kway Teow

Stir-fried flat rice noodles with prawns, eggs, bean sprouts, and Chinese sausage, cooked in a wok over high heat.

Assam Laksa

A tangy and spicy noodle soup made with tamarind-based broth, mackerel fish, and thick rice noodles.
